WELCOME TO THE OFFICIAL WEBSITE FOR THE COVINA POLICE DEPARTMENT

The City of Covina is located in Los Angeles County within the San Gabriel Valley. It is located approximately 20 miles east of downtown Los Angeles. The City is primarily a residential community with numerous elementary, intermediate and high schools. The City also contains commercial, light industry areas, Foothill Transit bus and Metrolink rail services. The City is approximately seven square miles with a resident population of approximately 50,000.
